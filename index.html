Walking through a door into another world

I entered the coding world in February 2023, whilst sitting and pondering my future, what would it look like? How could I move forward if I was stuck in a cloud of my own thoughts and imagination?
I began to write all my ideas and aspirations down, hoping for some inspiration or direction. Coding wasn't the first thing that came to me but after long deliberation and thought I had decided I wanted a career that would allow my ideas and imagination to flow. I was finally set on coding.
the only thing I was missing was the ability and skills to create these ideas. This dilemma had finally given me enough motivation to become fully immersed into the coding world.
Once I had made this decision, my ideas began to explode and run wild, the realisation that I could finally allow my imagination to flow into something worthwhile.
I had found my new freedom into another world.
I have a good sense of what I want, and I finally know what trajectory I am heading in. I no longer feel like I'm lost in the clouds with nothing to aim for, wandering with my eyes closed.
I am continuously putting energies into my own development, especially into the coding world.
This journey is a continuous learning experience. It seems that the more I learn and understand, the more I realise just what possibilities and opportunities are waiting for me.
The dedication and hours spent learning these new languages are definatly an investment in my future. The skills are invaluable, going through this door opens a lot more than I could have ever imagined.
