Walking through a door into another world

I put a lot of thought into what I wanted before deciding on what to study, coding wasn't the first thing that came to me but after long deliberation and thought I had decided I wanted a career that would allow my ideas and imagination to flow. I was finally set on web development.
The only thing I was missing was the ability and skills to create these ideas. Very fast I became fully immersed into the coding world.
My ideas began to explode and run wild, the realisation that I could finally allow my imagination to flow into something worthwhile.
I had found my new freedom into another world.
I have a good sense of what I want, and I finally know what trajectory I am heading in. I no longer feel like I'm lost in the clouds trying to figure out what path I should take.
This journey is a continuous learning experience. It seems that the more I learn and understand, the more I realise just what possibilities and opportunities are waiting for me.
The dedication and hours spent learning these new languages are definatly an investment in my future. The skills are invaluable, going through this door opens a lot more than I could have ever imagined.
